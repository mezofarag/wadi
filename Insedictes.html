Insecticides


Emperor

An effective natural pesticide in eliminating leaf and fruit worms, fruit worms in tomatoes at a rate of 80 cm / 100 liters of water.



Nilfos

Phosphorous insecticide used to combat many Roundworm insect pest Rodent in potatoes at a rate of 1 liter per feddan



Promed

A natural bleach that is effective in eliminating leafworms, fruits, and cotton leafworms in cabbage, at a rate of 60 grams per feddan



Cezamac

Systemic insecticide to control many sucking gnawing insects, whitefly, on tomatoes at a rate of 25 gm, 100 liters of water.



Glory

Insecticide to prevent moulting of worms, worms of grape fruits, at a rate of 40 cm per 100 liters of water.



Sunuron

Insecticide to prevent moulting of worms and cotton leafworms in potatoes at a rate of 160 ml per feddan.



Niloxam

A systemic insecticide to control many aphid-sucking insects on potatoes at a rate of 80 grams per feddan

Wadi El Nil Co.

Wadi El Nil Co. was established in 1985. Wadi El Nil for Agricultural Development is a permanent partner in the Egyptian market. It deals with agrochemicals,including, biostimulants , bio-pesticides , natural pesticides , public health products, liquid fertilizers, plant stimulants and enhancers, seeds, and sprayers. We keep up with the newest developments in those fields.
