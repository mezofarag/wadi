Abamectin is a widely used insecticide and anthelmintic. Abamectin, is a member of the Avermectin family and is a natural fermentation product of soil dwelling actinomycete Streptomyces avermitilis. Abamectin, differs from Ivermectin, the popular member of the Avermectin family, by double bond on C-22-25

We keep up with the newest developments in those fields.
